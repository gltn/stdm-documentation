‘People – land’ relationships can be expressed in terms of persons, group of people and institution (parties) having a right to parcel, garden, or structure (spatial units). In STDM, this right is called Social Tenure Relationship (STR).

The Social Tenure Relationship has its own table that stores each person's relationship to a spatial unit. Most importantly, it also gives you an option to choose the type of social tenure relationship between the person and the spatial unit. In addition, you can upload a supporting document that can be any document you think is useful for informational purpose and as evidence.

The STR table has two modules that are used to create, edit, and search STR.
