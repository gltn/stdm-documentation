Changing the Current Profile

As you can easily create profiles in the Configuration Wizard, switching between profiles is achieved in just two clicks. These profiles are created and customized by the Configuration Wizard. Once you create a new profile, you can then switch to it.

To change the current profile, follow the steps below.

1. Open the Options module as explained in Options topic.

2. At the top of the Options module, click on the drop down menu labelled Set current profile. It holds list of profiles in your Configuration.

3. At the bottom of the module, click on the Apply button ().

This will lead to a success message as shown below.

You can also see the changed profile in QGIS status bar as shown below.

Changing the current profile leads to the following.

      • Removal of the STDM layers

      • Change of Entity Menu items by the newly loaded profile entities.

      • Change of the Current STDM Profile text in QGIS status bar.

      • Change of tables participating in social tenure relationship.

      • Change of list of tables that are accessed throughout STDM.

      • All STDM modules reload to capture the newly loaded profile and database tables.
