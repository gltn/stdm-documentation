Working with GPS

It is advisable to use GPS device in the open field to avoid obstruction from buildings and trees, which hinder visibility of satellites when collecting data. Working with GPS in an obstructive environment reduces the accuracy of the final data. Depending on the GPS device, a GPS receiver must be locked to signals from at least 3- 4 satellites to be able to calculate the position. The higher the signal strength in terms of satellite signals, the higher the accuracy/reliability.
